About Me

I am a graduate of Agriculture from the University of Ilorin, Kwara State. Since 2019, I have worked as a Customer Care Executive at CrusaderSterling pensions. I am based in Lagos State, Nigeria.

I began my tech journey with Utiva in 2019 where I took a data incubator course in Data analytics and went on to Information and Data analytics foundation to study Data Science in their fellowship program in 2020. During my time at IDAF, I was exposed to websitedevelopment during my capstone project. I began to develop interest in Software development despite starting out in Data Science.

When I am not coding or working, I enjoy cooking, hiking, watching movies and hanging out with my best friends.
